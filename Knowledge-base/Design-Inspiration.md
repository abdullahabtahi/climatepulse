## 📐 9. For the Subagent Context (Screenshot Analysis)

When pasting screenshots to agents, ask them to analyze for:
1. **Spacing rhythm** — 4pt or 8pt grid?
2. **Typography hierarchy** — How many levels? Weight contrast ratio?
3. **Color role mapping** — Primary / Neutral / Error / Warning token usage
4. **Elevation model** — Shadow vs blur vs border to separate layers?
5. **Icon system** — Outlined vs filled, consistent stroke width?
6. **Component density** — Cards per viewport, information density score
